About Us

Power to Become is an initiative born from lived experience in rural Kenya. Growing up, our founder faced firsthand the challenges of limited access to quality healthcare, education, and agricultural resources. These obstacles inspired a vision: to empower young people in marginalized communities by providing mentorship, digital skills, and exposure to transformative opportunities.

Why these programs? We focus on healthcare, education, and agriculture because these are the pillars that shape livelihoods in rural Africa. By addressing these areas, we aim to break cycles of poverty and unlock the potential within every young person.

Our Vision: To ignite a movement where data-driven innovation transforms communities, enabling young people to thrive and become agents of change. As we scale, our goal is to create a ripple effect‚Äîempowering not just individuals, but entire communities across Africa.

Scaling Impact: As we expand, our solutions are designed to be adaptable and scalable, enabling us to transform not just local communities but the entire continent of Africa.
